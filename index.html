What we do

We're students who are interested in learning new skills everyday. Here's some of what we'd like to work on

Programming

We love discovering the possibilities and applications of computer science. To us it's something more than just the number of languages we know.

The Rules

Licensing, usage, copyrights, and business galore! We want our apps to be more than side projects. To do that, we'll need to know what we can do, how we can do it, and what we can't do.

Fundamentals

Knowing a language isn't enough. Learning fundamental programming concepts will help us write cleaner and quicker code.

Hardware

It's not all about software. We're interested in learning to work with existing hardware, along with newer technologies like Virtual Reality.

Teamwork

Working with others is much harder than hanging out. Teamwork is not a new skill, but definitely one that we can continue to develop and improve.

Design

Products need to be functional and beautiful. Colors, layouts, and fonts are just some of the topics that we're learning more about.
